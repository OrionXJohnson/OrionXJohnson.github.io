Understanding Rotation

Rotation in volleyball is the way players move through positions after gaining service. Knowing each rotation position is critical for both offense and defense.

Movement Patterns

Successful rotation requires fluid movement, anticipation, and communication. Focus on staying square to the net and moving quickly into your next position.

Leftside and Rightside Transitions

These drills show how left and right side players move into position during offensive rotations.

Player Roles in Rotation

  • Front row: attack and block
  • Back row: defense and serve receive
  • Setter position: transition to the net and set the ball
  • Libero: defensive specialist, floor coverage

Rotation Tips

  • Communicate before and after each play.
  • Move early to avoid late rotations.
  • Keep spacing between players clear.
  • Adjust quickly based on opponent attack.
