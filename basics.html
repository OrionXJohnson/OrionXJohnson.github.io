Key Terms

  • Serve: The first contact of the rally
  • Pass: The first contact after the serve
  • Set: The second contact used to position the ball for attack
  • Attack: A hit intended to score a point
  • Block: Defensive play at the net
  • Defend: The contact made to prevent the opponent from scoring
