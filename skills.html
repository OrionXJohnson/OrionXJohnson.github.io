Skills Training

This page is focused on skill development using images and videos from Gold Medal Squared. The text is a mix of original coaching notes and references to Gold Medal Squared training techniques.

Passing & Reception

Learn the proper platform and footwork for consistent passing. Gold Medal Squared emphasizes reading the server and moving early to get into position.

Setting Technique

Strong setting mechanics are essential. Focus on hand placement, body balance, and quick footwork, with drills that build reliable sets under pressure.

Serving

Serving is the first offensive action in every play. Proper technique and consistent placement can put pressure on the opponent while building the server’s confidence.

Approach Technique

A strong attack begins with the approach. Footwork, timing, and angle are critical for making powerful, well-placed hits.

Attacking

Attacking is how points are finished. Training includes arm swing mechanics, timing, and shot selection to hit with power and accuracy.

Blocking

Blocking is the first line of defense at the net. Effective blocking uses footwork, timing, and hand positioning to slow or stop the opponent’s attack.

Defense & Floor Coverage

Defense requires quick reactions and efficient movement. The drills shown here help players cover the court while maintaining strong defensive posture.

  • Practice targeted serving
  • Balance offense and defense
